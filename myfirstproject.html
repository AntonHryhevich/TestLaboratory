Top 10 strongest Mobs in Minecraft

Top 10: Zombie

Zombie is considered a very weak mob. He is very slow and does very little damage to players.

The drops from zombies are very useless in comparison to other mobs as he drops very often the rotten flesh, which is often useless.

Top 9: Skeleton

Skeletons do decent damage with their bow. All you need to do is, to dodge their arrows.

The drops from skeletons are somewhat useful. They drop bones and sometimes even a bow.

Top 8: Spider

Spiders are very fast creatures and they can even climb walls. They are vulnerable to the enchant Bane of artropods.

The drops from spiders are useful. They drop string and spider eyes, so you can craft a bow or brew potions.

Top 7: Creeper

Creepers are very dangerous creatures. They do a lot of damage on explosion. You can block the damage by using a shield or by pushing them away when they explode.

The drops from creepers are not very useful. They drop gunpowder or nothing, if they explode.
